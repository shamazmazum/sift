How-to

First of all, convert your image to a two-dimensional array of double floats where elements vary from zero to one. Than convert the array to a Gaussian scale space representation using gaussian-scale-space. I recommend leaving keyword parameters as is. Read the original paper if you want to know their meaning. After that, use either descriptors(the fast way) or keypoints and maybe describe-point to get keypoints and (optionally) descriptors. You can use find-matches to match the descriptors against another set of descriptors from another image.
